## About the Collection
This collection is about Gertrude Meendering who lived from 1918 to 2007 and the experiences she had going to college in the 1930s, getting married, having children, and learning to live on her on her own again after the death of her husband in 1978. This collection was created for the course SLIS:5030 Information Organization.
